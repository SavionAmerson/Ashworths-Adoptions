WHO ARE WE?

Ashworth's Adoptions is a non-profit dog shelter located in St. Louis, MO. Our team is made up of volunteers and animal specialists who work to bring in dogs from the streets and give them new homes.

OUR MISSION

With the rising rate of euthenization and animal abuse, we at Ashworth's devote our resources to finding abandoned or injured dogs left on the streets. Our mission is to bring them in, treat them if needed, and find new, loving homes for them.

HOW YOU CAN HELP

Any donations of any amount are greatly appreciated. 100% of donations go directly towards our resources for helping dogs. You can also become a temporary member of our team and become a volunteer! The more hands we have helping our cause, the more dogs we can save from the streets!
